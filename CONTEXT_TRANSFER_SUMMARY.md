## 📊 **What's New in Phase 3**

### **Calculus Calculator** 📈
- **Differentiation:** Power rule, trig (sin, cos, tan), exponential (e^x), logarithmic (ln x)
- **Integration:** Power rule, trig, exponential, logarithmic
- **Step-by-step solutions** shown
- Supports simple expressions and sums

### **Statistics Calculator** 📊
- **Linear Regression:** Calculate y = mx + c
- **R² Value:** Goodness of fit
- **Correlation Coefficient:** Relationship strength
- Handles comma-separated data input

### **Probability Calculator** 🎲
- **Binomial Probability:** P(X=k) for n trials
- **Union Probability:** P(A∪B) = P(A) + P(B) - P(A∩B)
- Shows combinations (nCk)
- Results in decimal and percentage

### **Ratio & Proportion** ⚖️
- **Simplify Ratios:** Reduce to lowest terms using GCD
- **Solve Proportions:** a:b = c:x, find x
- Cross multiplication method
- Clear step-by-step explanations
