---

### 2️⃣ **Sequence & Series Calculator** 📈
**Location:** Utilities → Sequences

**Features:**
- ✅ Arithmetic Progression (AP)
  - Find nth term
  - Calculate sum of n terms
- ✅ Geometric Progression (GP)
  - Find nth term
  - Calculate sum of n terms
  - Sum to infinity (when |r| < 1)
- ✅ Sum of First N Natural Numbers
- ✅ Sum of Squares (1² + 2² + ... + n²)
- ✅ Sum of Cubes (1³ + 2³ + ... + n³)

**Examples:**
```
AP: a=2, d=3, n=10
→ 10th term = 29
→ Sum = 155

GP: a=2, r=3, n=5
→ 5th term = 162
→ Sum = 242

Sum of 1+2+...+100 = 5050
Sum of 1²+2²+...+10² = 385
Sum of 1³+2³+...+5³ = 225
```

**Use Cases:**
- Class 10-12: Series problems
- BS: Mathematical sequences
- Competitive exams

---

### 3️⃣ **Decimal ↔ Fraction Converter** 🔄
**Location:** Utilities → Dec↔Frac

**Features:**
- ✅ Decimal to Fraction
  - Automatic simplification
  - Mixed number format
- ✅ Fraction to Decimal
  - Precise conversion

**Examples:**
```
0.75 → 3/4
0.333... → 1/3 (approximate)
2.5 → 5/2 or 2 1/2 (mixed)
3/4 → 0.75
7/8 → 0.875
```

**Use Cases:**
- Class 5-8: Fraction basics
- Class 9-10: Decimal conversions
- Everyday calculations

---

### 4️⃣ **Matrix 3×3 Calculator** 🔢
**Location:** Utilities → Matrix 3×3

**Features:**
- ✅ Addition (A + B)
- ✅ Subtraction (A - B)
- ✅ Multiplication (A × B)
- ✅ Determinant (det(A))
- ✅ Transpose (Aᵀ)

**Examples:**
```
Matrix A:        Matrix B:
[1  2  3]       [7  8  9]
[4  5  6]   +   [4  5  6]
[7  8  9]       [1  2  3]

Result (A + B):
[8  10  12]
[8  10  12]
[8  10  12]

Determinant of A = 0
```

**Use Cases:**
- Class 11-12: Linear algebra
- BS: Matrix operations
- Engineering calculations

---

### 5️⃣ **Complex Numbers Calculator** 🔢
**Location:** Utilities → Complex

**Features:**
- ✅ Addition: (a+bi) + (c+di)
- ✅ Subtraction: (a+bi) - (c+di)
- ✅ Multiplication: (a+bi) × (c+di)
- ✅ Division: (a+bi) ÷ (c+di)
- ✅ Conjugate: z̄
- ✅ Modulus: |z|
- ✅ Polar Form: r∠θ and re^(iθ)

**Examples:**
```
(3+4i) + (1+2i) = 4 + 6i
(3+4i) × (1+2i) = -5 + 10i
|3+4i| = 5
3+4i → 5∠53.13° or 5e^(i0.9273)
```

**Use Cases:**
- Class 12: Complex number problems
- BS: Electrical engineering (AC circuits)
- Signal processing
