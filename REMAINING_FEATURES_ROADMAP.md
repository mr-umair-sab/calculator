### 5️⃣ **Sequence & Series Calculator** ⭐⭐⭐⭐
**Usage:** 40% students (Class 10 to BS)
**Difficulty:** Easy-Medium
**Time:** 2 days

**Features to Add:**
- **Arithmetic Progression:**
  - nth term: aₙ = a + (n-1)d
  - Sum of n terms: Sₙ = n/2(2a + (n-1)d)
  - Find missing terms

- **Geometric Progression:**
  - nth term: aₙ = arⁿ⁻¹
  - Sum of n terms: Sₙ = a(rⁿ-1)/(r-1)
  - Sum to infinity: S∞ = a/(1-r)

- **Special Series:**
  - Sum of first n natural numbers
  - Sum of squares: 1² + 2² + ... + n²
  - Sum of cubes: 1³ + 2³ + ... + n³

**Why Important:**
- Common in exams
- Pattern recognition
- Financial calculations
